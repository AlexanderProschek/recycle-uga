Roosevelt@UGA Sustainability Project

  In 2018, UGA sent 5,474 tons of waste to landfills and recycled only ~1,200 tons of materials (... and we have recycling bins across the entire campus).

  Not only does recycling waste save material that could end up in a landfill or in one of many bodies of water, it also saves copious amounts of energy compared to creating products from virgin materials (those that haven't been recycled).
